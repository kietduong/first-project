His first year there, the college's football team won the NCAA national championship and went on an unprecedented winning streak. He promptly celebrated the champioship by running aorund the liquor store where he worked. Naked. He currently lives in Portland and attends Epicodus where he intends on learning coding and programming while he figures out what he wants to do with his life. His favorite color is blue.
